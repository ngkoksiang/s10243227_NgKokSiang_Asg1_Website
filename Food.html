Japanese Cheesecake!

Thanks to the Japanese method of whisking the egg whites separately before integrating them by hand for aeration, they have a more fluffy, sponge-like texture than traditional cheesecake but retain that beloved rich, creamy richness.

Blueberry Pancake

Thick and fluffy blueberry pancakes that are really simple. Golden brown, melt-in-your-mouth, and loaded with blueberries. A pancake (also known as a hot-cake, griddlecake, or flapjack) is a flat cake that is frequently round and thin that is made from a starch-based batter that may also contain eggs, milk, and butter. It is cooked on a hot surface, such as a griddle or frying pan, and is frequently fried in oil or butter. It is a variety of batter-baked bread.
