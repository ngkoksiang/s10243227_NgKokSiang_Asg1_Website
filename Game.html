League of Legends

Played since 2011

Riot Games created and released League of Legends, sometimes known as League, a multiplayer online battle arena computer game in 2009. The creators of Riot were motivated to create a standalone game in the similar genre by the Warcraft III custom map Defense of the Ancients.

Hearthstone

Played since 2017

Blizzard Entertainment created and released the free-to-play digital collectible card game Hearthstone. Hearthstone, which was first titled Heroes of Warcraft, expands on the lore of the Warcraft series by making use of the same objects, people, and artifacts.
